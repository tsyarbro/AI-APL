Pilot Program at a Glance

This section outlines the strategic foundation of the APL Pilot Program. It covers the core objective, the specific areas of focus within its scope, and the key metrics we will use to measure its success and impact on our teams and leadership.

Objective

To equip Agile People Leaders (APLs) with the knowledge, tools, and partnerships to deepen their understanding of technical work and AI, enhancing their ability to coach and grow our technical talent effectively.

Roles & Responsibilities

This pilot's success relies on a strong partnership between several key roles. This section clarifies the primary responsibilities for each group involved, ensuring alignment and a collaborative environment throughout the program.

Agile People Leaders (APLs)

Actively participate in all learning modules, shadowing sessions, and coaching activities to build new skills.

Tech Leads

Partner with APLs for joint check-ins, provide context on technical work, and share knowledge openly.

Program Team

Facilitate learning, track progress against metrics, gather feedback, and iterate on the program.

Leadership (Sponsors)

Champion the pilot, review outcomes and feedback, and support strategic decisions about scaling.
